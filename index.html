My passion for perfumes began with a fascination for the art of fragrance creation. Over the years, I have accumulated a wealth of knowledge about various perfume notes, brands, and trends. This understanding allows me to guide customers in selecting fragrances that best suit their personal styles and preferences.

My experiences in perfume has been rewarding and enriching. They have equipped me with a unique blend of skills and knowledge, enabling me to thrive in the dynamic world of fragrances. About Me

I am Jasmine Yap, an entrepreneur who embarked on my business journey at the impressive age of 18. With a passion for fragrances and a keen business acumen, I've built a successful fragrance company that continues to captivate customers with unique and enchanting scents. From a young age, I was fascinated by the world of fragrances. My interest quickly turned into a passion, leading me to explore the art and science of scent creation. At just 18, I took the bold step of founding my own fragrance company, driven by a desire to share my creations with the world.

Throughout my career, I've achieved significant milestones. My brand has launched multiple successful fragrance lines, each known for their unique compositions and high quality ingredients. My innovative approach and commitment to excellence have garnered numerous awards and recognitions in the fragrance industry.
